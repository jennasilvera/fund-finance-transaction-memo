# 2. Facility Type

The facility is structured as a senior secured hybrid subscription / NAV revolving credit facility.

The subscription component is supported by eligible unfunded investor commitments, capital call rights, and related collateral. The NAV component provides additional monitoring support through eligible fund asset value, LTV testing, and NAV floor requirements.

The subscription borrowing base shall be calculated as the sum of eligible unfunded investor commitments multiplied by applicable advance rates, subject to concentration limits, investor exclusion events, and other eligibility criteria.

| Ineligible / Defaulting Investor | 0% |

## Borrowing Base Exclusions

The following commitments may be excluded from the borrowing base:

- Defaulting investors
- Investors subject to bankruptcy or insolvency proceedings
- Investors in breach of material fund obligations
- Sanctioned or restricted investors
- GP / affiliate commitments
- Investors with unresolved side letter restrictions affecting capital call enforceability
- Investors otherwise deemed ineligible under the credit agreement

## Affirmative Covenants

The borrower shall:

- Deliver borrowing base certificates
- Deliver NAV and portfolio reports
- Deliver compliance certificates
- Maintain fund existence and authority
- Maintain pledged collateral and collection accounts
- Provide notice of investor defaults
- Provide notice of material adverse changes
- Provide notice of material amendments to fund documents
- Comply with applicable laws and fund documents

## Negative Covenants

The borrower shall not, without required lender consent:

- Incur prohibited additional indebtedness
- Amend material fund documents in a manner adverse to lenders
- Release or impair collateral
- Change investment strategy materially
- Make restricted payments during a default or borrowing base deficiency
- Permit liens on collateral other than permitted liens
- Merge, consolidate, or dissolve relevant borrower entities
